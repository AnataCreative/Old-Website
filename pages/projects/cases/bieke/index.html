A new website for Bieke Dejonghe.

Together with Bieke, we created a website to boost her business. The goal is to lower the hurdle for potential clients to make an appointment and to boost work inquiries.

Anata Creative was responsible for everything, from wireframing, to designing and development. By using the low-profile Perch CMS, this site is blazing fast but also very easy to maintain.
